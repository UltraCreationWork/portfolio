About Us

Experts In Architectural Services in Rourkela

Rahul Sinha & Design Alliance (RSDA) is an experienced firm of architects. RSDA was formed in 2019 with its design innovation center at Rourkela & Ranchi, with the thought of practicing sustainable, dynamic and green architecture. We believe in doing work of the highest possible quality, and lavish care on both fundamental design ideas as well as minute details.

RSDA works in the areas of Sustainability, Architectural consultancy, Affordable housing, Mixed-Use Design, Apartment, Commercial building design, Institutional buildings, Industrial buildings, Hospital design, Interior Design, Landscape Design and related areas.

At RSDA, we explore and develop architectural, interior and landscape design for various type of projects in context responsive design approach. RSDA applies creative design solutions to difficult design issues, whether it is planning a building on a limited site or providing a design within the parameters of a potentially restrictive construction budget. The firm provides the expertise and attention to each project as it deserves and requires.

"We Create spaces by exploring your goals, Priorities, Lifestyles and Tastes with an aesthetics sense"

We always believed that you deserve the home that you always wanted. Through research, innovation, highly creative solutions with years of experience that embody our clients' necessities, we advance in this field. Our diverse and multi-talented team is intense, passionate, and client-focused delivering 100% results. To work hard, nurture, and cultivate the right ideas is what we've always considered.

Transparency, Quality and Trust defines our company and we strongly believe in it.
